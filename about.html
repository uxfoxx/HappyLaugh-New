About Us

Nestled in the picturesque hills of Ella, Sri Lanka, Ceylon Happy Laugh offers a serene retreat for travelers seeking comfort and adventure. Our establishment is more than just a place to stay; it's an experience that combines the warmth of Sri Lankan hospitality with the natural beauty of the region.

Located at the 4th Mile Post, Yahalawela, Passara Road, our property provides easy access to Ella's renowned attractions, including the Demodara Nine Arch Bridge and Little Adam's Peak. Whether you're a solo traveler, a couple, or a family, Ceylon Happy Laugh is designed to cater to your unique needs, ensuring a memorable stay amidst nature's splendors.

Local Amenities

To enhance your stay, we provide a range of amenities:

  • Local Restaurants:

    Savor authentic Sri Lankan cuisine prepared with fresh, local ingredients at our on-site restaurant. Our expert chefs craft traditional and contemporary dishes, offering a delightful culinary experience.

  • Explore Ella's Dining Scene:

    Beyond our in-house restaurant, Ella boasts a variety of eateries catering to diverse tastes. From cozy cafés serving aromatic Ceylon tea to vibrant restaurants offering international flavors, you can indulge in everything from local rice and curry to wood-fired pizzas and fresh seafood. Popular spots include Café Chill, Ella Flower Garden, and Matey Hut, each providing a unique dining experience.

  • Nature Surroundings:

    Immerse yourself in the tranquil beauty of Ella's lush landscapes, right at our doorstep. Enjoy breathtaking views, fresh mountain air, and easy access to scenic trails leading to iconic locations like Little Adam's Peak and Nine Arches Bridge.

  • Haze Recreation Facility:

    Located at Ceylon Happy Laugh, the Haze Recreation Facility is the ultimate spot for relaxation and entertainment. Whether you're looking to unwind with signature cocktails, enjoy live music, or dance the night away, Haze offers a vibrant atmosphere perfect for socializing. The venue also hosts events, themed parties, and exclusive gatherings. Planning something special? Haze is available for private bookings, making it the ideal choice for birthdays, corporate events, and celebrations.
